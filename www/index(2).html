FAQs

Press the button for Info you Need!

  • What's in Stats Table?
  • What should I input in Settings?
  • Max Loss:
    Maximum Loss encountered during simulation of this time.
  • Expected Profit per Bet Round:
    The average profit of this test per round. If you test for 1000 rounds, it will be sum of total profit of 1000 round divided by 1000.
  • Prob. of Win:
    Number of win rounds / Number of rounds in this simulation.
  • Prob. of Max Loss more than 10,000:
    Number of rounds which loss is > 10,000 / Number of rounds in this simulation.
  • Total Bet Amount:
    Sum of total bet amount of all rounds in this simulation. If you test for 1000 rounds, this is sum of the total amount you bet in 1000 rounds.

How to Setup Simulation

Here is what you should input:

  • Base Bet:
    Initial bet amount at the start of the bet
  • Max Bet:
    Maximum amount you can bet
  • Increase Rate at Loss:
    If you put in 2, you will double you bet when you lose
  • Bet Odds:
    European bet odds. Under bet odds = 2 assumption, if you bet $1 and you win, you'll get $2 and profit is $1
  • Win Chance:
    Probability of win, please look at your betting sites(ex: freebitcoin) for it
  • Stop Loss Amount:
    When your bet lose more than this amount, 1 round ends and start a next round in simulation
  • Stop Profit Amount:
    When your bet win more than this amount, 1 round ends and start a next round in simulation
  • Bet Times:
    The maximum number of times you bet in 1 round
  • Simulation Round:
    Number of rounds you use in this simlation
